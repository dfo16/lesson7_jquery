Beef and Broccoli Stuffed Sweet Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 4 (8 oz) sweet potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil, divided
  • 1 cup chopped red onion
  • 3/4 cup drained and chopped bottled roasted red bell peppers
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 2 cups of broccoli florets
  • 2 oz of medium cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped greens

Instructions

Rub sweet potatoes with 1 1/2 teaspoons oil; pierce several times with a fork. Microwave at HIGH 12 to 15 minutes or until potatoes are tender.

Heat a large skillet over medium-high. Add remaining 1 1/2 teaspoons oil; swirl to coat. Add onion and red bell peppers; cook 5 minutes or until tender, stirring frequently. Add garlic, and cook 3 minutes, stirring frequently. Stir in chili powder, 1/4 teaÂ­spoon salt, cumin, and ground red pepper. Add beef; cook 6 minutes or until browned, stirring to crumble.

Preheat broiler to high.

Heat broccoli according to package directions; stir into beef mixture. Arrange potatoes on a baking sheet. Partially split potatoes lengthwise; fluff the flesh with a fork.

Top potatoes evenly with beef mixture, remaining 1/4 teaspoon salt, and cheese.

Broil 30 seconds or until cheese melts.

Sprinkle with green onions.
